The Best Movies According to Alex

My top 3 movies

Fight Club

This is a movie I put off for so long because I thought it gloried violence, until I actually watched it and realised it wasn't anything like that.

Se7en

I love the tension in this movie, the story of the antagonist and find it thought provoking.

The Green Book

A movie my husband chose for us to watch, I hadn't heard of it before. Viggo Mortenson was spectacular in the movie, I think he's a world class actor.
